Every Picture Project Development

Reflection

After reviewing the peer feedback I received, I realized that my project could benefit from diving deeper into the narrative and personal connection behind my content. For future iterations, I plan to take a more personal approach by inviting viewers into my workspace allowing them to explore my desk and interact with various items to learn about their purpose and meaning.

To develop this idea, I created several sketches of how the final website might look. I’m also exploring different JavaScript and CSS techniques to enhance the storytelling aspect of the project. Some potential features include:

  • Using a carousel to display a sequence of images in a more dynamic and engaging way.
  • Implementing image mapping so users can click on specific objects on my desk to reveal more information.
  • Adding subtle animation, such as my avatar guiding viewers as they scroll through the page.
  • Refining the hover overlay transitions for smoother and more visually consistent alignment.

These changes aim to make the project feel more interactive, meaningful, and uniquely tied to my own creative process.

Concerns & next steps:

I can’t wait to get started on this project. I think my only concern is making sure I can apply the skills I’ve learned in class while keeping the overall experience cohesive and story-driven rather than choppy.
